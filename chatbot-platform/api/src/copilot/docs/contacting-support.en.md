# Contacting support

Axentrio support is email-based.

**General questions / billing / setup issues:** support@axentrio.com.

**Response times:**
- Essential — within 2 business days
- Pro — within 1 business day
- Enterprise — within 4 business hours (and priority phone if your contract includes it)

**What to include in a support email:**
- Your tenant name (visible in the top-left of the portal)
- A description of what's broken or unclear
- Screenshots help — particularly of the *AI Bot & Content* page or the failing widget

**For sales / Enterprise pricing:** sales@axentrio.com.

**For data privacy / GDPR requests:** privacy@axentrio.com.

**Self-help first:** before emailing, try:
- *Help & FAQ* in the sidebar — covers the most common questions
- The AI Platform Assistant (Pro+) — answers most "how do I" questions instantly

**Status page:** https://status.axentrio.com — check here if multiple things look broken; could be a platform-wide incident rather than something specific to your tenant.
