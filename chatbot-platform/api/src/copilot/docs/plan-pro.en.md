# Pro plan

Pro adds everything you need to scale beyond the basics — booking integrations, branding, richer Lead handling, and the AI Platform Assistant.

**Everything in Essential, plus:**
- Cal.com bookings (the bot books meetings directly into your calendar)
- Hide the "Powered by Axentrio" attribution on your widget
- Custom widget appearance (colours, position, welcome message)
- Custom Lead fields + lead routing rules
- The AI Platform Assistant (the Copilot you're talking to right now — answers your questions about how Axentrio works)
- Higher daily LLM call cap
- Priority email support

**Upgrading from Essential:** *Settings → Billing → Upgrade to Pro*. Charged prorated for the remainder of the billing period. Pro features become available immediately on success.

**Trialing Pro features:** new signups get a Pro trial automatically — see *Settings → Billing* for trial end date.
