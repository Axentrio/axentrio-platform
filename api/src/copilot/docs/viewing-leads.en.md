# Viewing leads

When the bot collects a visitor's contact details, the entry shows up in *Lead Capture → Leads*.

**What a lead row contains:** name, email, phone (if given), source (which channel), the chat session it came from, and when it was captured.

**Filtering:** filter by source (widget, Facebook, WhatsApp, etc) or date range. Search by name or email.

**Export:** click **Export** to download all visible leads as CSV — useful for importing into your CRM.

**Lead row clicks:** click a lead to see the full conversation transcript that captured them. Useful for context before you reach out.

**Why no leads yet?** if your widget is installed and people are chatting but no leads appear, check that *Lead Capture* is **enabled** in the sidebar (it can be off if you don't want the bot asking for contact info). Also: leads are only captured when the visitor actively gives an email or phone — the bot won't grab it from greetings.
