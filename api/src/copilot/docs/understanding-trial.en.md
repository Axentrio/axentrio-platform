# Understanding your trial

New signups get a free **Pro trial** automatically — every Pro feature is unlocked, no credit card required up-front.

**Trial duration:** 14 days from signup.

**What works during trial:**
- Every Pro feature is active: Cal.com bookings, widget branding, custom Lead fields, the AI Platform Assistant
- Daily LLM call cap is at Pro level
- Full Lead Capture inbox

**Trial end:**
- 3 days before end, you get an email reminder.
- On the trial-end day, you're prompted to add payment to continue with Pro.
- If you don't add payment, your tenant **automatically drops to Essential** (not free) — the bot keeps working, but Pro-only features stop.

**Adding payment mid-trial:** *Settings → Billing → Add payment method*. Card is charged when the trial ends, prorated to the next billing date.

**Cancelling during trial:** open *Settings → Billing → Cancel*. You keep Pro until the trial naturally ends, then drop to free.
