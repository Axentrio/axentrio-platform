# Essential plan

Essential is the entry tier — everything you need to put a bot on your website and capture leads.

**What's included:**
- One bot, one knowledge base
- Website widget (chat bubble on your site)
- Lead capture (visitors give email/phone, you get a Lead row)
- Lead Capture inbox + CSV export
- Daily LLM call cap suited to small-to-medium traffic
- Email support

**What's NOT included (these need Pro):**
- Cal.com bookings
- Hide the "Powered by Axentrio" attribution
- Custom widget appearance (colours, branding)
- Custom Lead fields and routing rules
- The AI Platform Assistant (this Copilot)

You can upgrade to Pro from *Settings → Billing* at any time — the change applies immediately and you're prorated for the rest of the month.
