# Essential vs Pro

The short version: Essential is "bot + leads", Pro is "bot + leads + bookings + branding + AI assistant".

**On Essential you have:**
- The bot answers questions from your knowledge base
- Visitors give contact info → Lead row appears in the inbox

**Upgrading to Pro unlocks:**
- **Cal.com bookings** — bot books meetings directly into your calendar
- **Widget branding** — your colours, your welcome message, hide our attribution
- **Custom Lead fields + routing** — extend the Lead schema, route by source
- **AI Platform Assistant** — Axentrio's own AI helps you operate the platform (this Copilot)
- **Higher daily LLM cap** — less risk of hitting limits on busy days
- **Priority support** — replies in hours, not days

**Cost:** Pro is roughly 2× Essential. Worth it if you're routinely booking meetings or want a branded experience.

**Try before buying:** new signups get a Pro trial automatically — use it to see which Pro features matter to your business.
