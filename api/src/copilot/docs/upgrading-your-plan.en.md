# Upgrading your plan

You can upgrade from Essential to Pro yourself in the portal. Upgrades to Enterprise are a conversation with the team.

**Self-serve (Essential → Pro):**
1. Open *Settings → Billing*.
2. Click **Upgrade to Pro**.
3. Confirm the prorated charge (you pay for the rest of the current billing period at Pro's daily rate).
4. Stripe checkout pops up — confirm payment.
5. Pro features become available within seconds. Refresh the page if Pro-only sidebar items (Bookings, AI Platform Assistant) don't appear immediately.

**Enterprise:** email sales@axentrio.com or book a call via *Help & FAQ*. The team will set up a tailored contract and switch your tier.

**Mid-month upgrades** are prorated automatically — you're not charged twice and your next invoice reflects only the Pro portion forward.

**Cancelling an upgrade:** you can downgrade back to Essential anytime from *Settings → Billing*. The downgrade applies at the start of your next billing period (you don't lose Pro mid-month).
