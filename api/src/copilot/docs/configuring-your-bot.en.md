# Configuring your bot

Your bot's behaviour is shaped by three things, all under *AI Bot & Content*:

1. **Brand voice.** The tone the bot uses — formal, friendly, technical, casual. Pick a preset or write your own. The bot uses this for every reply.

2. **Custom instructions.** Specific rules the bot must follow: "Always quote prices in EUR", "Refer enterprise enquiries to sales@", "Never promise installation in under 48 hours". Keep these tight and unambiguous.

3. **Knowledge documents.** Upload PDFs, FAQs, policies, or product spec sheets under *AI Bot & Content → Knowledge*. The bot grounds its answers in these — without them it falls back to generic AI behaviour.

Changes take effect within seconds. Test by opening your widget in an incognito tab and asking the bot a question the new instructions should change.
