# Integrations overview

Axentrio integrates with three classes of external tool: **channels** (where the bot can talk), **calendars** (where it can book), and **CRM/webhook** (where Leads can flow downstream).

**Channels:**
- Website widget (built-in, every tier)
- Facebook Messenger (every tier)
- Instagram DMs (every tier)
- WhatsApp Business (every tier)
- Telegram (every tier)

**Calendar:**
- Cal.com (Pro+; for booking meetings during chats)

**CRM / outbound:**
- n8n workflows (Pro+; route Lead captures into your CRM, Slack, email — any n8n node)
- Webhooks (Pro+; raw POST to your URL on each Lead capture)

**Roadmap:**
- HubSpot direct connector (was on the v1 ship list; deferred to v2)
- Stripe customer linking (post-booking checkout)
- Zapier (community-driven)

**Where to find them:** all under *Social Media* (for channels), *Bookings → Integrations* (for Cal.com), and *Settings → Integrations* (for webhooks and n8n).

**Status of each integration:** the *AI Bot & Content* status card on the dashboard shows which integrations are connected vs. not.
