# Uploading knowledge

Your bot grounds its answers in the documents you upload under *AI Bot & Content → Knowledge*.

**Supported formats:** PDF, DOCX, TXT, Markdown, plain text.

**Size limits:** 20 MB per file on Essential, 50 MB on Pro, 200 MB on Enterprise. File-upload limits beyond document chunking apply to image and audio attachments separately.

**What to upload:**
- Product spec sheets, pricing pages, FAQs, service descriptions
- Internal policies the bot should follow ("we offer 30-day refunds", "engineers serve a 25km radius")
- Anything a customer might ask that isn't on your public website

**What NOT to upload:**
- Confidential employee or customer data — the bot may quote it back
- Old or contradictory drafts — the bot will mix them and answer confusingly

After upload, processing takes 30–60 seconds. The document appears in the list with a status indicator; when it shows "Ready", the bot is using it.
