# Exporting your data

You own your data. You can pull it out of Axentrio anytime.

**Leads CSV:** *Lead Capture → Leads → Export*. Downloads every Lead row with name, email, phone, source, capture date, and any custom fields.

**Chat transcripts:** *Chats → click any session → Export transcript*. Plain text or JSON. For bulk export of all chats, email support@axentrio.com — we generate a ZIP of all chat sessions and email a download link within 24h.

**Knowledge documents:** they're still your originals — re-download them from *AI Bot & Content → Knowledge → click a doc → Download*.

**Bot configuration:** brand voice, custom instructions, and integration settings are not currently exposed as an export. Take a screenshot of your *AI Bot & Content* page for record-keeping; full settings export is on the roadmap.

**GDPR data subject request:** if a visitor asks for their data, email support@axentrio.com with the email or phone in question. We compile the data and respond within 30 days per regulation.

**After cancellation:** exports are available for 30 days after subscription end. Email support to confirm retention status if you need data later.
