# Handoff to a human operator

Sometimes the bot can't handle a conversation — the question is too specific, the visitor is upset, or they explicitly ask to talk to a person. When that happens, handoff hands the chat to one of your teammates.

**How it triggers:**
- The visitor asks for a human ("speak to someone", "can I talk to a manager")
- The bot detects it can't answer confidently
- Custom instructions tell the bot to escalate on specific topics (refunds, complaints, etc)

**What the operator sees:** the chat appears in *Inbox* with a "handoff" badge and the visitor's recent messages. An operator clicks the chat, types a reply, and the visitor sees it appear in real time. The bot stays out of the conversation until the operator releases it back.

**Setting up operators:** *Settings → Team → Invite teammate*. Each invited user gets their own login. Anyone on the team can pick up handed-off chats.

**Notifications:** operators get a desktop notification when a handoff arrives. Optional email notifications for chats that wait more than 5 minutes.

Handoff is available on every tier.
