# The AI Platform Assistant

The AI Platform Assistant is an in-portal AI that answers your questions about Axentrio itself — how to configure your bot, install the widget, find leads, connect Cal.com, understand your plan.

**Where it lives:** bottom-right of every portal page. Click the chat bubble to open the drawer. Stays open across navigation; closes on demand.

**What it can answer:**
- "How do I install the widget?"
- "What plan am I on, and when does it renew?"
- "How many leads have I captured this week?"
- "Why isn't my bot replying?"
- "How do I connect Cal.com?"

**What it can NOT do (in v1):**
- Take actions on your behalf — it can answer "how to connect Cal.com" but won't connect it for you
- See data from other tenants (only ever your own)
- Read or share private credentials, API keys, or Stripe details

**Tier:** Pro and Enterprise only. Essential admins see a Pro lock on the launcher.

**Cost cap:** a daily message cap prevents runaway usage. If you hit the cap, the launcher tells you when it resets.
