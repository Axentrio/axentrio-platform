# Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel anytime from *Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription*. No phone calls, no retention scripts.

**What happens when you cancel:**
- Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period — you don't lose features mid-month.
- At period end, your tenant drops to the **free** internal state — bot is paused, widget stops responding, no new leads captured.
- Your data is retained: leads, chat history, knowledge documents, settings.

**Reactivating later:** open the portal, *Settings → Billing → Reactivate*. Pick a tier, complete checkout. The bot resumes, the widget starts answering again, all your existing data is still there.

**Permanent deletion:** if you want your data deleted entirely, email support@axentrio.com from your tenant admin email. We respond within 5 business days with a deletion confirmation.

**Refunds:** we don't issue refunds for partial months — but you keep access until the period ends, so cancel anytime in the current month and you're not paying for time you won't use.
